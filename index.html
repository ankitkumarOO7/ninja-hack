Event Desciption

The event consists of 2 parts. Both of them will be having challenging coding questions that the participants have to solve and earn points. But unlike other code-thons our event is different. The participants will be exposed to a wide range of programming languages some common while others not. To make the competition more interesting and challenging we will be introducing our cryptic language in which they have to solve very basic questions. The event is not just about solving some mind-boggling questions but how well you know the fundamentals and can apply them. The questions will be ranging from easy to hard so both beginners, as well as pro coders, will enjoy solving it.

Event Detail

Level 1

This level consists of 28 questions pertaining to common programming languages like Python, Java, C, C++. Each language will have 7 questions. The questions will be designed as such to test the knowledge of the fundamentals.

Level 2

This level consists of 20 questions. The languages provided to solve them will be old/unknown (less popular). Each of them will be having 5 questions. This will increase the difficulty quotient of the event.

Level 3

In this level we will introduce our cryptic languages. The participants will have to identify how the language works then accordingly solve the questions to get the desired output. The no. of questions will be 10.

Bonus

This is a kind of a bonus round where teams have the chance of increasing their points and rank on leaderboard. Total 40 questions will be released in intervals.
